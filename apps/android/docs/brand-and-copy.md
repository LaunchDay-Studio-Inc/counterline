## Who This Is For

### Ambitious Intermediates (~1200–1600)
Want their first serious, cohesive repertoire. Tired of learning random openings without a plan. Need a system that helps them retain moves and understand the ideas behind them.

### Competitive Club Players (~1600–2000)
Preparing two reliable weapons for tournament play. Want deviation coverage so they know what to do when opponents surprise them. Value the drill system for retention before events.

### Titled Grinders (~2000–2300+)
Looking for engine-validated lines with clear post-opening plans. Want to see the proof data. Use the prep pack generator and practice modes to sharpen before events.

### Elite Lab Users (any rating, data-curious)
Want access to proof matrix data, specialist tuning details, and deep multi-PV analysis. Interested in the methodology as much as the lines themselves.

## Performance Claims (Evidence-Gated)

Only the following performance statements may be used:

- CounterLine Combined scored 52.50% against Stockfish 18 on the published fixed suite (LOS ~93%)
- The Black specialist scored 53.75% on the Caro-Kann Classical exit, with 3 wins and 0 losses as Black in 20 games
- The Vienna Gambit exit position is nearly 100% winning for White regardless of engine
- Results are from 40-game matches with ±23 Elo error bars
- The null-wrapper control scored exactly 50%, confirming gains come from the specialist
- LOS values of 92–93% indicate a positive trend, not yet proven at the conventional 95% threshold

## Required Disclaimers

All public-facing surfaces (app, store listing, website, book) must include:

1. CounterLine is derived from Stockfish and distributed under GPL-3.0
2. Results are specific to the published fixed suite and tested conditions
3. Engine-vs-engine results do not automatically transfer to human play
4. 40-game matches have statistical error bars of approximately ±23 Elo

## Release Notes (v1.0.0)

CounterLine v1.0.0 — First Release

Two engine-tested opening lines for serious study:
- White: Vienna Gambit Accepted (C29)
- Black: Caro-Kann Classical 4…Bf5 (B18)

Features:
- Move-by-move line walkthrough with annotations
- Spaced-repetition drills (fill-in-blank, choose-move, flashcard)
- Line-lock sparring, deviation sparring, and play-from-tabiya mode
- Elite analysis pane with multi-PV, compare-your-move, explain-last-move
- Prep session generator with exportable cheat sheets
- Personal coach with daily workout and readiness scores
- Exam mode, tactical motifs, transition trainer
- Annotated model games from real engine-vs-engine matches
- Full offline operation — no internet required
- Open source (GPL-3.0) with full PGN proof files
