## CounterLine: An Engine-Tested Opening Repertoire

**Two weapons. One system. Every claim verifiable.**

You don't need ten opening systems. You need two that work.

CounterLine gives you exactly that: one prepared line for White (the Vienna
Gambit Accepted) and one for Black (the Caro-Kann Classical). Both were
selected from a field of 28 candidates through automated screening — and
both were validated in head-to-head matches against Stockfish 18 on a
published fixed suite.

**What you get:**

- **10 moves to memorise per colour** — no decision trees, no "you can
  also try..." paralysis. One path. Follow it.
- **Annotated model games** — see how the lines play out in real
  engine-vs-engine matches, move by move.
- **Plans and patterns** — know what to do *after* the memorised line
  ends. The exit position is where the game begins.
- **Deviation handling** — when opponents deviate, you know what to play
  and why.
- **A drill system** — fill-in-the-blank exercises, position quizzes,
  and deviation drills to lock the lines into memory.
- **Full transparency** — every PGN, every FEN, every reproduction
  command is published. Check the results yourself.

**Who this book is for:**

Ambitious club players who are tired of winging the opening. Coaches who
want concrete study material. Engine testers who want to see how a
specialist wrapper architecture works. Anyone who values evidence over
assertion.

**What this book does NOT claim:**

That you'll beat Stockfish in a general game. That this repertoire works
everywhere. That 40-game matches prove anything beyond a statistically
meaningful trend. We tell you exactly where the evidence stops.

*Honest engineering. Transparent boundaries. Real results.*
