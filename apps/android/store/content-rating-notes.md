## Notes for Reviewer
CounterLine is a chess training app with no user-generated content, no social features, no in-app purchases, no ads, and no network access. All content is pre-loaded chess opening data derived from open-source engine testing. The app includes the Stockfish chess engine for on-device position analysis only.
