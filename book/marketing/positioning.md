# Positioning

> All statements below are governed by `content/claims_manifest.json`.
> Forbidden phrases must never appear in any marketing surface.

## Core Positioning Statement

**CounterLine** is an **engine-tested opening repertoire system** that gives
ambitious club players two concrete weapons — one for White, one for Black —
selected through automated screening of 28 candidate openings and validated
in controlled matches against Stockfish 18.

## What CounterLine IS

- A fixed-line repertoire: one prepared sequence for each colour
- Engine-tested: validated in 40-game matches under published conditions
- Reproducible: every game, setting, and command is public
- Derived from Stockfish: a Python wrapper over the open-source engine
- Honest: the book tells you exactly where the evidence stops

## What CounterLine Is NOT

- A general-purpose engine improvement
- A way to "beat Stockfish" in arbitrary games
- Something proven at 95% statistical confidence (yet)
- A guarantee that human players will replicate engine results

## Target Audience

| Segment | How They Use It |
|---------|----------------|
| Ambitious club players (1400–2000) | Memorise two sharp lines; stop winging the opening |
| Coaches and trainers | Assign as repertoire study material with built-in drills |
| Engine testers / CS students | Study the wrapper architecture and reproduce matches |
| Opening repertoire collectors | Add two tested weapons to the shelf |

## Competitive Differentiation

| Traditional Opening Book | CounterLine |
|--------------------------|-------------|
| Author says "this is good" | Machine-tested, results published |
| Many options, reader chooses | One path per colour — no choice paralysis |
| Theory changes, book ages | Tested against a specific engine version with reproducible results |
| No proof | PGN files, FENs, commands — verify it yourself |

## Voice and Tone

- **Direct.** No hedging where evidence exists. No embellishment where it doesn't.
- **Honest.** State limitations upfront. Readers respect transparency.
- **Practical.** Every claim leads to something the reader can do.
- **Confident, not arrogant.** "This scored 52.50%" not "This crushes everything."

## Price Positioning

This is a specialist product. Not mass-market, not bargain-bin. Position it as a
premium study tool for players who are serious about opening preparation — priced
in line with quality opening repertoire books (€15–25 digital, €25–35 print).
