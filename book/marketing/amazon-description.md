**Stop choosing between twenty opening systems. Start playing two that have been machine-tested.**

CounterLine is not a traditional opening book. It does not give you options. It gives you **one prepared line for White and one for Black** — selected from 28 candidates through automated engine screening, then validated in controlled matches against Stockfish 18.

**As White, you play the Vienna Gambit Accepted.** Ten memorised moves lead to a position where White has a lasting structural advantage — a position so favourable that White wins approximately 95% of games from it in engine testing, regardless of which engine plays.

**As Black, you play the Caro-Kann Classical (4...Bf5).** Ten memorised moves lead to a solid defensive fortress. In proof-matrix testing, CounterLine's Black specialist scored 53.75% against Stockfish 18 from this position — with three wins and zero losses as Black.
