HOW IT WORKS
CounterLine gives you exactly two openings to master. No bloated database. No cramming 40 variations. You learn one White line and one Black line, with built-in spaced-repetition drilling so you actually retain the moves.

The entire repertoire is engine-tested and reproducible. The published PGN files let you verify every claim yourself with the reproduction commands included in the open-source repository.

ENGINE-TEST RESULTS
CounterLine Combined scored 52.50% against Stockfish 18 on the published fixed suite (LOS ~93%). The Black specialist scored 53.75% on the Caro-Kann Classical exit, with 3 wins and 0 losses as Black in 20 games. Results are from 40-game matches with ±23 Elo error bars. LOS values of 92–93% indicate a positive trend, not yet proven at the conventional 95% threshold.

The null-wrapper control scored exactly 50%, confirming gains come from the specialist.
